Endangered Species ⚠️

Species on the edge — conservation awareness matters

🌍

Over 40,000 species are currently threatened with extinction. Climate change, habitat loss, and poaching are the leading causes. Awareness and action can reverse this trend.

Amur Leopard

CRITICAL

World's rarest wild cat. Conservation efforts have slowly increased numbers.

📍 Russian Far East ~100 left

PRIMARY THREATS

  • Poaching
  • Habitat loss

Vaquita Porpoise

CRITICAL

The world's most critically endangered marine mammal. Near extinction.

📍 Gulf of California ~10 left

PRIMARY THREATS

  • Bycatch in fishing nets
  • Habitat degradation

Hawksbill Sea Turtle

CRITICAL

Ancient mariners threatened by shell poaching and coastal development

📍 Tropical oceans worldwide ~8,000 nesting females left

PRIMARY THREATS

  • Shell trade
  • Habitat loss
  • Climate change

Javan Rhino

CRITICAL

The world's rarest rhino species. They are found in only one national park in the entire world.

📍 Ujung Kulon, Indonesia ~76 left

PRIMARY THREATS

  • Natural disasters
  • Poaching
  • Habitat loss

Sumatran Orangutan

CRITICAL

Highly intelligent apes threatened by rapid deforestation in Sumatra.

📍 Sumatra, Indonesia ~14,000 left

PRIMARY THREATS

  • Deforestation
  • Palm oil industry

Saola

CRITICAL

Known as the "Asian Unicorn," it is so rare that it has only been documented in the wild a few times.

📍Annamite Mountains, Vietnam/Laos ~Unknown (dozens)

PRIMARY THREATS

  • Snares set by hunters
  • Habitat fragmentationy
  • Infrastructure development

Cross River Gorilla

CRITICAL

The most endangered great ape in Africa. They are extremely wary of humans and inhabit rugged terrain.

📍Nigeria-Cameroon border ~300 left

PRIMARY THREATS

  • Deforestation
  • Poaching
  • Genetic isolation

South China Tiger

CRITICAL

Considered "functionally extinct" in the wild, as it hasn't been seen outside of captivity for decades.

📍China ~100 left

PRIMARY THREATS

  • Extinction in the wild
  • Loss of prey
  • Fragmented habitat
